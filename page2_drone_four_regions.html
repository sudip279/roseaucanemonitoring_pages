◆ STANDARDIZED METHODOLOGY

Consistent Drone Survey Protocol Across All Sites

📐
RTK GPS Ground Control
Customized stake-mounted targets developed for soft wetland substrate. Upgraded to larger targets (6 at 24" + 6 at 36" stakes) for high water conditions. RTK base runs minimum 4 hours per survey.
🚁
UAV Platform Selection
Industrial multispectral drone for MRD and Rockefeller. Phantom 4 (natural color) for shallow-water Atchafalaya site. Platform selection driven by site accessibility and water depth constraints.
🧠
Deep Learning Classification
U-Net semantic segmentation model applied to processed orthomosaics. Agisoft Metashape used for orthomosaic production. Training samples collected through drone-based field sampling to overcome wetland accessibility limitations.
